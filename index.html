About us

Zana Specialty pharmacy is a closed-door long-term care specialty pharmacy. It was
founded on the firm belief that the current pharmacy-healthcare facility relationship
can be improved

We want to be more than just a pharmacy. We want to be part of your team! For
this reason, our closed-door pharmacy is not open to the community and does not
function in a retail community capacity. This has allowed us to focus on only
delivering services to healthcare facilities and practices such as Skilled Nursing
Facilities (SNF), Residential Care Facilities for Elderly (RCFE)
